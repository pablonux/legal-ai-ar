# Work Item — Jurisprudential Trends

**Created**: 2026-03-25
**Status**: Draft
**Feature**: Analytics & Profiles

## User Story

As a **lawyer**, I want **to visualize how the judicial interpretation of a legal topic or statute has evolved over time** so that **I can identify shifts in jurisprudence, emerging doctrines, and the current direction of the courts**.

## Context

- **Current state**: The dashboard shows general timelines of ruling volume, but no topic-specific trend analysis exists.
- **Target state**: A trend analysis view where the user selects a legal topic, keyword, or statute article and sees:
  - **Timeline**: Rulings over time, colored by outcome tendency or court.
  - **Interpretation shifts**: AI-detected turning points where the dominant interpretation changed.
  - **Key rulings**: Landmark decisions that marked a shift, highlighted on the timeline.
  - **Court comparison**: How different courts trend on the same topic.
- **Complements**: The interactive timeline planned in Phase 3 roadmap. This WI specifies the analytical/AI layer on top.
- **Reference**: vLex trend analytics, Lexsphere precedent evolution tracking.
