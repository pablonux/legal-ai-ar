# Work Item — Share Research

**Created**: 2026-03-25
**Status**: Draft
**Feature**: Collaboration

## User Story

As a **lawyer**, I want **to share a ruling, a search result set, or a research folder with a colleague via link or email** so that **I can collaborate on case preparation without requiring them to repeat my research**.

## Context

- **Current state**: No sharing capabilities. Each user's experience is completely isolated.
- **Target state**: Share actions available on:
  - **Ruling detail**: "Share" button generates a direct link (recipient must have an account) or sends an email with ruling summary and link.
  - **Search results**: Share current search query + filters as a link.
  - **Research folders**: Share a folder as read-only or read-write with specific users.
  Shared items appear in the recipient's "Shared with me" section.
- **Access control**: Integrates with the role-based access system planned in Phase 2. Sharing respects user permissions.
- **Reference**: Sapphire Legal shared research, HighQ collaboration, vLex sharing.
