# Work Item — Team Workspace

**Created**: 2026-03-25
**Status**: Draft
**Feature**: Collaboration

## User Story

As a **team lead at a law firm**, I want **a shared workspace where my team can collaborate on a case with shared folders, annotations, and research notes** so that **everyone on the team has access to the same research base and we avoid duplicating effort**.

## Context

- **Current state**: All user data is individual. No team or organization concept exists beyond admin role.
- **Target state**: Organizations/teams can be created. Within a team workspace:
  - Shared research folders visible to all team members.
  - Shared annotations on rulings (team members see each other's highlights and notes).
  - Activity feed showing team research activity.
  - Team admin can manage members and permissions.
- **Prerequisites**: Role-based access (Phase 2 roadmap), Research Folders (WI research-folders), Annotations (WI annotations).
- **Reference**: HighQ team workspaces, Sapphire Legal collaborative workspace, Plexus team features.
