# Work Item — Comments on Rulings

**Created**: 2026-03-25
**Status**: Draft
**Feature**: Collaboration

## User Story

As a **lawyer working in a team**, I want **to add comments on a ruling that are visible to my colleagues** so that **we can discuss the relevance and interpretation of a ruling directly in context without switching to email or chat apps**.

## Context

- **Current state**: No commenting system. Annotations (WI annotations) are personal. No inter-user communication within the platform.
- **Target state**: On the ruling detail page, a comments panel (sidebar or section below) where team members can:
  - Post comments (text, optionally referencing a specific passage).
  - Reply to comments (threaded).
  - Mention team members (@user).
  - Resolve/archive comment threads.
  Comments are scoped to the team workspace — only team members see them.
- **Prerequisites**: Team Workspace (WI team-workspace), user identity and roles.
- **Reference**: Syllo team comments, Litigation Ready collaborative notes, Google Docs commenting model.
