# Work Item — Legal Drafting Assistant

**Created**: 2026-03-25
**Status**: Draft
**Feature**: Advanced AI Assistant

## User Story

As a **lawyer**, I want **the assistant to help me draft legal documents (demands, contestaciones, recursos, dictamenes) with proper format, legal language, and embedded citations** so that **I can produce high-quality legal writing faster while ensuring jurisprudential support**.

## Context

- **Current state**: The assistant can answer legal questions with citations, but cannot produce structured legal documents with Argentine procedural formatting.
- **Target state**: A drafting mode where the user specifies the document type, jurisdiction, key facts, and desired arguments. The AI generates a draft following Argentine legal writing conventions, inserting citations to indexed rulings. The user can iteratively refine the draft through chat.
- **Templates**: Common Argentine legal document structures (demanda, contestacion, recurso extraordinario, recurso de casacion, dictamen).
- **Reference**: Sapphire Legal AI document generation, BRYTER workflows, Caseway CaseForm.
