# Argentine Legal Branches (Ramas del Derecho)

Use this taxonomy to classify legal questions and rulings.

## Public Law

| Code | Branch | Key Statutes |
|---|---|---|
| PUB_CONST | Constitutional law | CN 1994, Treaties with constitutional hierarchy (art. 75 inc. 22) |
| PUB_ADMIN | Administrative law | Ley 19.549 (administrative procedure), Ley 26.944 (state liability), Ley 25.164 (public employment) |
| PUB_PENAL | Criminal law | Código Penal (Ley 11.179), Ley 23.737 (narcotics), Ley 26.388 (cybercrime) |
| PUB_PROC_CIV | Civil procedure | CPCCN (Ley 17.454) |
| PUB_PROC_PEN | Criminal procedure | CPPF (Ley 27.482, accusatory system) |
| PUB_TRIB | Tax law | Ley 11.683 (tax procedure), Ley 20.628 (income tax), Ley 23.349 (VAT) |
| PUB_INT | Public international law | Vienna Conventions, UN Charter, bilateral treaties |

## Private Law

| Code | Branch | Key Statutes |
|---|---|---|
| PRIV_CIVIL | Civil law | CCyCN (Ley 26.994): persons, family, property, obligations, contracts, liability |
| PRIV_COM | Commercial law | CCyCN + Ley 19.550 (corporations) + Ley 24.522 (bankruptcy) + Ley 17.811 (capital markets) |
| PRIV_LAB | Individual labor law | Ley 20.744 (LCT): employment contract, working hours, compensation, termination |
| PRIV_LAB_COL | Collective labor law | Ley 23.551 (unions), Ley 14.250 (collective bargaining agreements) |
| PRIV_SEG | Insurance law | Ley 17.418: insurance contract, risk, premium, claim |
| PRIV_PI | Intellectual property | Ley 11.723 (copyright), Ley 24.481 (patents) |

## Social and Family Law

| Code | Branch | Key Statutes |
|---|---|---|
| SOC_FAM | Family law | CCyCN arts. 401-723: marriage, divorce, filiation, adoption, alimony, custody |
| SOC_PREV | Social security law | Ley 24.241 (SIJP), Ley 26.425 (SIPA/ANSES), pensions |
| SOC_NINEZ | Children's rights | Ley 26.061 (comprehensive protection), CDN (constitutional hierarchy) |
| SOC_AMB | Environmental law | Ley 25.675 (General Environmental Law), art. 41 CN |
| SOC_CONS | Consumer law | Ley 24.240 (Consumer Protection), art. 42 CN |
